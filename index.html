01 · About

About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Ocean University. My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and water science.

I am particularly interested in transforming complex, noisy, nonlinear, and nonstationary environmental observations into reliable scientific knowledge and actionable decisions.

My work combines deep learning, signal decomposition, physical constraints, causal inference, reinforcement learning, and multi-objective optimization. I have participated in research on dissolved oxygen forecasting, phosphorus regulation, basin resource allocation, hydro-wind-solar uncertainty, and intelligent agricultural information systems.

04 · Projects

Research & Engineering Projects

National Undergraduate Innovation Project · Principal Investigator

Deep Reinforcement Learning and Cooperative Game Theory for Transboundary Basin Resource Allocation

Developed an integrated assessment–optimization– allocation framework by combining planetary boundaries, carbon footprints, cooperative game theory, and a deep-reinforcement-learning-enhanced multi-objective evolutionary algorithm.

First-author Research

Robust Dissolved Oxygen Prediction and Control-oriented Regulation in Complex Lakes

Integrated improved dung beetle optimization, variational mode decomposition, CEEMDAN, CNN, and iTransformer, and designed a stepwise temperature-oriented regulation strategy.

First-author Research

Physics-informed Reliable Prediction of Dissolved Oxygen in Complex River Networks

Derived physical prior parameters from the Streeter–Phelps equation and developed a hybrid BiTCN–iTransformer model with temporal enhancement, physical constraints, and interpretability analysis.

First-author Research

Physics-informed Causal Framework for Phosphorus Regulation in Wastewater Treatment Systems

Combined ASM2d process mechanisms, double machine learning, causal analysis, SHAP interpretation, and adaptive regulation to support reliable effluent phosphorus control.

Collaborative Research

Conversion–Management–Perception Framework for Hydro–Wind–Solar Uncertainty

Contributed to multi-source data processing, Copula modeling, multi-objective optimization, Pareto analysis, result interpretation, and manuscript preparation.

Collaborative Research

Reinforcement-Learning-Driven Multi-objective Scheduling of Cascade Reservoirs

Conducted model parameter tuning, multi-scenario simulations, Pareto-front analysis, and scientific visualization for adaptive reservoir scheduling.

Municipal Innovation Project · Team Member

Physics-Informed Neural Networks for Fluid Mechanics

Built and validated PINN models for rotating multiphase flow, aiming to reduce mesh dependence and improve prediction efficiency for flow instability thresholds.

Engineering Practice · Project Lead

Intelligent Precipitation–Farmland Monitoring Platform
